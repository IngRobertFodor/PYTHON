📱 SnapCall - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Summary: SnapCall does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All image processing happens entirely on your device.

1. Introduction

SnapCall ("the App") is a mobile application that uses your device's camera to scan and recognize phone numbers from images. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect any personal data.

Specifically, SnapCall:

  • Does NOT collect your name, email, or any personal information
  • Does NOT store or save any photos you take
  • Does NOT transmit images or phone numbers to any server
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking services
  • Does NOT contain advertisements
  • Does NOT require an account or login

3. Camera Usage

The App requires access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of capturing images of phone numbers. Camera images are:

  • Processed entirely on your device using Google ML Kit (on-device OCR)
  • Used only temporarily during the scanning process
  • Immediately discarded after phone numbers are extracted
  • Never uploaded, shared, or stored permanently

4. Phone Calls

When you tap a detected phone number, the App opens your device's native phone dialer. The App itself does not make calls directly – it simply passes the number to your phone's built-in calling function.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google ML Kit Text Recognition for OCR (Optical Character Recognition). This processing happens entirely on your device (on-device model). No data is sent to Google's servers for text recognition.

For more information about Google ML Kit's privacy practices, visit: Google ML Kit Terms

6. Data Storage

SnapCall does not store any user data. The App does not use local databases, cloud storage, or any form of persistent data storage for user content.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The App does not collect any information from any user.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
